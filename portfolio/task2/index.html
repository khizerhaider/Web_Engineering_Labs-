Meal Type Recipe
Breakfast

Classic Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 1¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions:

Mix dry ingredients. Combine wet ingredients separately. Mix together until just combined. Cook on a hot griddle until bubbles form, then flip.

Lunch

Grilled Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • Grilled chicken breast
  • Mixed greens
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Balsamic vinaigrette

Instructions:

Grill chicken until cooked through. Slice and arrange over mixed greens. Add vegetables and dress with vinaigrette.

Dinner

Pasta Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Pecorino Romano
  • Pancetta
  • Black pepper

Instructions:

Cook pasta. Fry pancetta. Mix eggs and cheese. Combine hot pasta with egg mixture and pancetta. Season with pepper.
